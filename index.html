Welcome To Manjyot Play School

MANJYOT-The name itself symbolises enlightenment of the mind. The concept of our school is “Learning to Play and Playing to Learn” .We emphasize basically on the Play – Metod Of Teaching .We are deeply influenced by the Montessori method of teaching and always try to impart the same to our tiny tots .Our system of Education emphasizes on the philosophy of child development and a rationale for guiding such growth ,which is based on two important developmental needs of our children.

Our Vision

The vision of our school is to develop and deliver Education to Anyone, Anytime, Anywhere. Our mission is to help young minds grow and learn in an amicable environment resulting in their holistic development. Our aim is to provide the experiences for Physical, Intellectual or Cognitive, Aesthetic, Emotional, Motor, Language, Sensorial and Social Developments of the child to cope with the ever growing challanges of life.
